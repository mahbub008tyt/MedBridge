How It Works

MedBridge is a B2B platform where Saudi hospitals, clinics, and wellness providers connect with Indonesian medical travel facilitators. Together, they create packages that serve Indonesian patients seeking quality treatment abroad.

Stakeholders

Saudi Stakeholders (S): Hospitals, Clinics, Wellness Centers, Hotels, Translators, Transport & Concierge Services.

Indonesian Stakeholders (I): Travel Agencies, Medical Tourism Facilitators, Patient Acquisition Platforms, Visa & Documentation Agents.
